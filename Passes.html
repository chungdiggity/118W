Festival Passes

Here you can find different pass types and which weekened that you and your friends would like to attend.

Pass types

General admission

General admission passes start off at $429.
GA allows entrance to the venue and day parking lots for all 3 days. GA pass holders may visit the camping areas.

  1. Limit two (2) passes.
  2. Wristband activation required.
  3. Orders ship via UPS Mail Innovations.
  4. Wristband void if tampered or removed.

VIP Passes

VIP passes price is $999.
Allows entrance to the Venue, VIP areas and Day Parking lots for all three days. VIP Pass holders may visit the GA Camping areas.
VIP Areas offer specialty food & drink vendors, air-conditioned restrooms, shaded seating areas and full cash bars. VIP Areas are located at the Five Peaks tents adjacent to the Main Stage and in the Rose Garden.

  1. Limit two (2) passes.
  2. Wristband activation required.
  3. Orders ship via UPS Mail Innovations.
  4. Wristband void if tampered or removed.
